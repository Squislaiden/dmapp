        -Therianthropes-
    -Traits-
  [Creature Type] - A therianthrope can be any land-based mammal (Eg, Werebear).
  [Appearance] - Gruffer look. Body Hair. Tough skin.
  [Resistance] - Immune to Non-Silvered Physical. Resist Magical Physical.
  [Darkvision] - 60 ft. of Darkvision.
  [Keen Senses] - Advantage on hearing and smelling.
  [Proficiencies] - Upgrade Perception and Intimidation.
  [Regeneration] - Regenerate 10 Hit Points at the start of your turn if you took nonmagical nonsilvered damage since the end of your previous turn.
  [Strength of Body] - On the initial transformation, increase one of two physical stats by 2; species specific.
  [Species Trait] - A trait unique to the species.

    -Abilities-
  [Were Form - An action to transform.
  [Multiattack - One claw and then Claw or Bite. Instances of Extra Attack add an extra Claw attack.
 (Claw) - Unarmed Melee Attack. 1d8 + Str Slash.
 (Bite) - Unarmed Melee Attack. 2d6 + Str Pierce. Constitution Save or Poisoned until end of next turn.
  [Movement] - +10 Movement Speed. 30 ft. Climb Speed.
  [Natural Armor] - 10 + Con Mod + Str Mod while not wearing armor or using a shield.
  [Gear] - Armor that has not been customized to flex will be torn off and left on the ground.

    -Weaknesses-
  [Full Moon] - +10 Speed, +2 AC, and +1d6 Claw and Bite.
 Upon nightfall, a Wisdom Save must be made every hour until Dawn. On a fail, the creature must transform into Were Form and seek to attack and eat beasts and humanoids, favoring humanoids. A save ends the compulsion until the next save.
  [Silver] - Silver bypasses all resistances and deals an additional 3d4 Acid Damage.
  [Wolfsbane] - Ingest: Weakens Immunity for 1 hour       Incense: Force Transformation.

    -Pack, Alpha, or Lone Wolf-
  [Pack] - Pack Tactics. +2 Athletics.
  [Alpha] - Pack Tactics. +3 Athletics. Leadership feat.
  [Lone Wolf] - +2 Stealth. Howl: Wisdom Save or Frighten. Half-proficiency uses that recharge on Long Rest.

    -Spreading and Removing the Curse-
  [Spreading] - Being born of a werewolf, failing two successive Bite Saving Throws, or 1 Bite on a Full Moon, causes a transformation at the next Full Moon.
  [Cure] - A paladin or cleric using Remove Curse must make a check. The check DC increases the closer the Full Moon is. The check uses Spellcasting Stat Modifier without Proficiency Bonus. Upcasting Remove Curse adds +1 to the roll for each additional Spell Level.
DC = 10 up to 25 at Full Moon. An unwilling target adds +5 DC.
 Creatures born weres can only be cured by Wish/Reincarnation.

    -The Crossover-
Therianthropes lose all sense of self and can sense Godsouls within 10 miles and are compelled to seek out and destroy them at all costs.

(God's Tear and The Crossover are unique events within the Terramet universe)
