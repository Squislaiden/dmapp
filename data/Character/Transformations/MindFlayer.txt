        ---Mind Flayer---
    -Traits-
  [Creature Type] - Aberration replaces normal type.
  [Resistance] - Resist Psychic.
  [Darkvision] - Darkvision up to 120 ft.
  [Telepathy] - Telepathy up to 120 ft.
  [Magic Resistance] - Advantage on saves vs spells and other magical effects.
  [Alien Intelligence] - Increase your Intelligence by 3.

    -Abilities-
  [Tentacle] - Unarmed Melee Attack. 1d10 + Int/Wis Psychic. Can grapple medium or smaller creatures.
  [Extract Brain] - As an Action, you can eat the brain of a humanoid that died less than 10 minutes ago. As a Reaction to killing a creature within 5 ft. of you, you can eat its brain. When you eat a brain, heal for 2d6 and learn one memory.
  [Mind Blast] - Action, can replace an attack. Select a creature within 60 ft. They must make an Intelligence Save or take 2d8 + Int/Wis psychic and be stunned for 1 minute. They can repeat the save at the end of their turn. Use one time after a Short Rest.
  [Detect Thoughts] - Can cast Detect Thoughts half PB times after a Long Rest.

    -Weaknesses-
  [Alien] - Unlikely to be helped or accepted and very likely to be attacked, especially by anyone that knows Mind Flayer lore.
  [Megalomaniac] - Incredibly Self-Important, Arrogant, and Ambitious.
  [Grey Matter Diet] - Must consume a humanoid brain at least once a week. A Mind flayer’s Intelligence drops by 1 on the eighth day and 1 more every day after. After consuming a brain, they instantly regain 2 Intelligence, but must wait and regain one per day afterwards to fully recover.
  [Hivemind] - If turned under the control of or dominated by an Elder Brain, you are compelled to serve their desires and goals.

    -Spreading and Removing the Morph-
  [Spreading] - Introduce a ceramorph tadpole into the victim’s head.
  [Cure] - The only way to cure it is with the Wish spell.

    -The Crossover-
 Mind Flayers don’t interact with the moon nor God’s Tear. They can, however, have strange and interesting interactions with the Eldritch Gods.

(God's Tear and The Crossover are unique events within the Terramet universe)
