        ---Godsoul---
    -Traits-
  [Creature Type] - Celestial in addition to normal type. A Godsoul can be any Avian or Reptile creature (Eg, Hawksoul, Komodosoul).
  [Resistance] - Immunity to Radiant. Vulnerable to Necrotic.
  [Divine Sense] - 30 ft. Blindsight. Can sense Fiends and Undead creatures within 30 ft.
  [Recharge] - As an Action, regain a Spell Slot of level equal to 1/3 your level or 2 charges of a Class Resource.
  [Strength of Mind] - On the initial transformation, a player chooses to increase a mental stat by 2.

    -Abilities-
  [Divine Smite] - On a weapon attack, deal an extra 2d6 Radiant. Regain use of this after a Short Rest.
  [Judgement] - As a Bonus Action, impose disadvantage on an evil creature’s next attack.
  [Shapechange] - While not in complete darkness, as an Action, polymorph into an avian/reptile form with the stats of a Blood Hawk/Giant Lizard.
  [Wings (Avian Only)] - As an Action, sprout a pair of feathery celestial wings for 1 minute, granting you a flight speed of 30 ft.
  [Resilience (Reptile Only)] - Gain Fire Resistance and are immune to Extreme Hot Weather.

    -Weaknesses-
  [See No Evil] - A Godsoul has a powerful impulse to stop any evil that it sees. A Wisdom save can ignore the impulse.
  [Full Moon] - The day of, before, and after full moon, the Godsoul is weakened. On the Full Moon, they lose all Godsoul traits for a 24 hour period. This does not happen during The Crossover.
  [Necrosis] - Taking necrotic damage causes Divine Sense, Resilience, Wings, and Spells to fail and attacks beyond the first don’t deal Strength or Dexterity damage until the end of your next turn.

    -As a Pair-
Godsouls are drawn to work together with another trusted Godsoul in pairs. While together, their urge to cleanse evil is more powerful.

    -Spreading and Removing the Blessing-
  [Spreading] - A forceful merging of an avian or reptile with a humanoid by a Godsoul or powerful Celestial over the course of 9 hours. This must be done on holy ground and will lure any fiends and werewolves, and alert vampires, within 20 miles. They may attempt to interrupt it.
  [Cure] - Bathe in the blood of an innocent for 9 hours, or sign a pact with an Archdevil.

    -The Crossover-
Godsouls lose all sense of self and can sense Therianthropes within 10 miles and are compelled to seek out and destroy them at all costs.

(God's Tear and The Crossover are unique events within the Terramet universe)
